Panchakarma

Panchakarma is a traditional Ayurvedic detoxification and rejuvenation therapy aimed at balancing the body's doshas (Vata, Pitta, and Kapha) and eliminating toxins (known as "Ama") from the system. The term "Panchakarma" translates to "Five Actions" or "Five Procedures," which refer to five therapeutic treatments used to cleanse the body.

Shirodhara
(Therapeutic Oil Dripping)

At our clinic, we offer Shirodhara, a soothing Ayurvedic therapy that involves pouring warm, medicated oil over the forehead. This treatment calms the mind, reduces stress, and promotes mental clarity. As you lie comfortably, the gentle flow of oil targets the "third eye" area, helping to alleviate anxiety and enhance overall well-being. Our skilled practitioners customize the oil blend to meet your individual needs, ensuring a relaxing and rejuvenating experience.
