About Daksh

Join us on a journey to discover the healing power of Ayurveda and experience a transformative path to balance and vitality. Your health is our priority, and we are here to guide you every step of the way.

Welcome to Our Ayurvedic Practice

Welcome to our Ayurvedic practice, where ancient wisdom meets modern healing. Led by Vaidya (Dr.) Aradhna Nautiyal, an esteemed Ayurvedic consultant and expert in Garbh Sanskar, our mission is to promote holistic health and well-being.

With a Bachelor of Ayurvedic Medicine and Surgery (BAMS) from UAU, Dr. Nautiyal is dedicated to offering personalized treatments that address the root causes of health issues. Her commitment to lifelong learning is evident through her attendance at numerous seminars, including those held by Vishw Ayurved Parishad, as well as specialized workshops on Nasya and Neti conducted by the Global Academy of Ayurveda and Yoga.

Dr. Nautiyal has also participated in the Ayurved Kaushlam events from 2021 to 2024, further enhancing her expertise in this timeless healing tradition.

In the spirit of Ayurveda, we believe in the harmony of mind, body, and soul, encapsulated in the Sanskrit shloka:

“सर्वं आयुरर्थं च”

(Sarvaṃ āyurarthaṃ ca)

“May all beings have longevity and prosperity.”

Our approach combines the richness of traditional practices with a focus on modern health needs, especially in the realm of Garbh Sanskar, where we nurture the health of mothers and unborn children.

Join us on this journey towards holistic well-being, where every treatment is a step towards balance and vitality.
